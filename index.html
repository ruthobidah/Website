Titan Park

Spring 2017 - Summer 2017

Titan Park application created with a very unique group of CSUF students. our team created for the GE Predix Hackathon for 2017 using the GE Predix API. The goal of the Hackathon was to create sustainable solutions for problem areas on our campuses where there were still inefficiencies.The final presentation was at the GE Headquaters in San Ramon. Our team included people with diverse skills and I worked on the design, UI, developing statistical models to be integrated in the application. Tides & Glory

Spring 2016

Strategy Game about pirates and Conquest done with Pygame

Story

The player is the captain of the ship in Tides and Glory. The captain is an ambitious strategist starting his/her conquest of the sea, in hopes of making the grandest storied sea adventure for fame and glory. The only way the captain can reach such iconic level is to accumulate as much wealth as possible, earn a name that would be sung in pirate lore for ages to come, and leave a trail of notorious deeds
