Titan Park

Spring 2017 - Summer 2017

Application created with a very unique group of CSUF students. Titan Park was a parking application created for the GE Predix Hackathon for 2017 using the GE Predix API. The goal of the Hackathon was to create sustainable solutions for problem areas on our campuses where there are still inefficiencies.The final presentation was at the GE Headquaters in San Ramon. Our team included people with diverse skills and I worked on the design, UI, developing statistical models to be integrated in the application. Working with my GE team was a wonderful experience on team work and project planning
