Tides & Glory

Spring 2016

Strategy Game about pirates and Conquest done with Pygame

Story

The player is the captain of the ship in Tides and Glory. The captain is an ambitious strategist starting his/her conquest of the sea, in hopes of making the grandest storied sea adventure for fame and glory. The only way the captain can reach such iconic level is to accumulate as much wealth as possible, earn a name that would be sung in pirate lore for ages to come, and leave a trail of notorious deeds
